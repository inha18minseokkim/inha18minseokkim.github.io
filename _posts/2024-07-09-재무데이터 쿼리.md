PSR = 주가/주당매출액 = 주가 * 주식수 / 매출액 = 시총 / 매출액

```lua
select
(select mrkt_price_tlam from tb_stk_li_stck_daily_info_m where itms_cd_nbr = '005930' and to_char(base_dttm,'yyyyMMdd') = '20240531')
/(select val_amt
from tb_stk_fncl_stmt_m where tp_id = 'K' and rpt_id  = '12' and acnt_id = '1000' and itms_cd_nbr = '005930')
```

ROE = 당기순이익/자기자본

```lua
SELECT (select val_amt
from tb_stk_fncl_stmt_m where tp_id = 'K' and rpt_id  = '17' and acnt_id = '1111' and itms_cd_nbr = '005930')
/(select val_amt
from tb_stk_fncl_stmt_m where tp_id = 'K' and rpt_id  = '11' and acnt_id = '8900' and itms_cd_nbr = '005930') * 100
```
